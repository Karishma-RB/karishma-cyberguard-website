What is the most common method used by attackers to spread ransomware?

A
Physical theft of devices
B
Phishing emails with malicious attachments
C
Brute force attacks on firewalls
D
Social engineering in person

Correct Answer: Phishing emails with malicious attachments

Ransomware is most commonly spread through phishing emails that trick users into opening malicious attachments or clicking on infected links. According to our knowledge bases, these emails often appear to come from legitimate sources and use social engineering tactics to bypass security measures.
